## B1 fix + test

The Reason exchange dead-ended on `accept` / `pattern_flag` (responder line is
a closing acknowledgement with no question) and on evaluator failure. Fix:
after the responder acknowledges, the tutor takes a fresh coach turn so the
lesson resumes (brief/03 §4 "the conversation resumes"). `soft_challenge` is
intentionally exempt — its targeted follow-up question already gives the pupil
a next move; a second turn would double-question. Proven by 4 deterministic
component tests (mocked `/api/reason/evaluate` + `/api/chat`): resume fires for
accept / pattern_flag / evaluator-failure, and does NOT for soft_challenge.
