# Parent/Pupil Privacy Information & Consent — DRAFT 🔒

> **DRAFT copy for the school + DPO to review, adapt, and approve.** Wording
> for children must be age-appropriate (ICO Age Appropriate Design Code). The
> school decides whether the lawful basis needs consent or is covered by public
> task with a privacy notice; this draft provides both a notice and an
> opt-in/objection form so the school can choose.

## Plain-English notice for pupils (Year 7–9 reading level)

> **Bridewell Classroom — what happens to what you type**
>
> In some lessons you'll work with an AI tutor that asks you questions to help
> you think. Here's the honest version of what happens:
> - Your teacher can see your messages and how you're getting on. That's so
>   they can help you, like they would looking over your shoulder.
> - The AI doesn't replace your teacher — your teacher is always in charge.
> - If you ever write something that makes us worried about your safety, your
>   teacher (or another trusted adult at school) will see it so they can help.
>   The AI won't make a decision about it — a person always does.
> - We keep your messages only for a short while, and you can ask for them to
>   be shown to you or deleted.
> - You join with a class code and your first name — you don't make an account.

## Notice for parents/carers

> Bridewell Classroom is an in-lesson AI tutor used under teacher supervision.
> It processes your child's first name and the messages they type during the
> activity to (a) coach them with questions, (b) give the teacher a live view
> of engagement, and (c) flag any safeguarding concern to school staff. The
> school is the data controller. Data is held in [region]; the AI model is
> operated by [provider] under contract. Chat is retained for [N days] then
> deleted; you may request access to or deletion of your child's data via
> [school contact]. Safeguarding records are kept under the school's
> child-protection retention schedule. Full detail: the school's privacy notice
> + DPIA. _DECISION: fill [region]/[provider]/[N]/[contact]._
